Gray is moving to NYC for his job with Sai (Hell's Kitchen, 50th St & 8th Ave). Target move-in: April 4, 2026 weekend.

**Why:** Job starts April 15, 2026. Needs to be settled before then.

**Search Criteria:**
- Budget: $2,000/mo (his share — open to roommates, any number)
- Max commute: 30 min to 50th St & 8th Ave, Hell's Kitchen
- Open to roommates (any number)
- Safe neighborhoods required
- Young professional vibe preferred (flexible on this)
- Open to NJ/Hoboken

**Top Neighborhood Picks (ranked):**

1. **Hell's Kitchen, Manhattan** — Walk to work, young/creative vibe, 2BR split ~$2,000–2,250/share. Best overall.
2. **Hoboken, NJ** — PATH ~20 min, most concentrated young professional vibe outside Manhattan, rooms ~$1,500–1,900. Best value.
3. **Astoria, Queens** — N/W train ~25 min, great food/bar scene, rooms ~$1,300–1,800. Best bang for buck.
4. **Long Island City (LIC), Queens** — E/M/7 train ~15–18 min, newer buildings, rooms ~$1,600–2,000.
5. **Murray Hill, Manhattan** — Classic young professional neighborhood, E/M ~20 min, rooms ~$1,800–2,100.
6. **Jersey City Downtown/Newport, NJ** — PATH ~25 min, cheapest option, rooms ~$1,300–1,700.

**Best Sites to Find Roommate Listings:**
- SpareRoom (best for NYC roommate listings)
- Facebook Groups ("NYC Roommates 2026", "Hoboken Roommates")
- Craigslist rooms/shared section
- StreetEasy roommate tab

**How to apply:** Have ready — proof of income, ID, references, first month + 1–2 months deposit.
