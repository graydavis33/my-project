# Apartment Search Notes

## Requirements
- Must be within 30 minutes of Hell's Kitchen, Manhattan
- Budget: $2,000/month max

## Top Neighborhood Picks (best value for budget)

### 1. Sunnyside, Queens
- Train: 7 train, ~20 min to Hell's Kitchen
- Studios: $1,600–$2,000
- Vibe: Quiet, affordable, underrated

### 2. Journal Square, Jersey City (NJ)
- Train: PATH, ~25 min to midtown
- Studios: $1,500–$2,000
- Vibe: Best space for the money

### 3. Astoria, Queens
- Train: A/C/N/W, ~20–25 min
- Studios: $1,800–$2,200
- Vibe: Great food scene, lively

## Other Options Considered
- Long Island City, Queens — E/M/7 train, ~15 min, studios $2,000–$2,400
- Washington Heights — A train, ~25 min, studios $1,600–$2,000
- Hoboken, NJ — PATH ~20 min, studios $2,000–$2,600

## Hell's Kitchen Direct
- Private studio: very rare at $2k, market is $2,800–$3,500+
- Room in shared apartment: $1,600–$2,000 (most realistic)

## Where to Find Listings
- Facebook Groups: "NYC Sublets", "Manhattan Sublets"
- Craigslist NYC housing
- Streeteasy.com
- Hotpads / Zillow
- Reddit: r/NYCapartments

## Safety Notes
- Verify sublets before sending money
- Meet in person or video call first
- Confirm primary tenant actually lives there
