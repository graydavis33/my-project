# Gray — Weekly Meal Schedule (work-prep)

Built 2026-07-23 from the revised Trader Joe's list. ~180-190g protein/day (whey fills gaps), slow carbs, T1D-friendly, lunches packable for work. Rotates 5 protein anchors: chicken, beef, tuna, sardines, eggs.

## Cook twice a week
- SUNDAY (big prep ~45 min): roast full pack chicken thighs · brown a batch ground beef · cook pot of brown rice · boil a dozen eggs · roast tray of sweet potatoes. Portion into containers.
- WEDNESDAY (10-min top-up): fresh batch of beef or chicken so the back half of the week stays fresh (cooked meat ~4 days in fridge).

## Daily rhythm
Black coffee AM (fasted is fine) → WORK LUNCH ~12-2 → afternoon snack → post-workout whey shake → dinner 2-3h before bed.

## 7-Day Schedule
| Day | Work Lunch (packed) | Snack | Shake | Dinner |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Mon | Chicken + brown rice + peppers/onions + cheese | Greek yogurt + berries | Whey + PB | Beef taco bowl (beef + black beans + corn tortillas + cheese) |
| Tue | Beef + rice + peppers bowl | 2 boiled eggs + jerky | Whey | Chicken thighs + sweet potato + peppers |
| Wed | Tuna + black beans + rice + EVOO/lemon | Cottage cheese + berries | Whey + banana | Beef + rice + peppers (fresh batch) |
| Thu | Chicken + rice + peppers | Turkey jerky + cheese | Whey | Sardines + 3 eggs + sweet potato (mashup) |
| Fri | Beef taco bowl | Greek yogurt + berries | Whey | Chicken + rice + peppers |
| Sat | 4-egg scramble + peppers + cheese + tortillas | Cottage cheese + berries | Whey | Beef + sweet potato + black beans |
| Sun | Tuna/sardine power bowl | Greek yogurt + berries | Whey + PB | Chicken + rice + peppers (part of prep cook) |

Backup: overnight oats (oats + Greek yogurt + whey + berries + PB) = breakfast or work snack any day.

## Packing for work
- 3-4 microwave-safe containers (glass lasts longest). Pack Mon-Wed lunches Sunday; Wed cook covers Thu-Fri.
- Freeze Thu/Fri lunches Sunday, move to fridge Wednesday.
- Shake to-go: dry whey scoop in shaker, add water at work (no leaks/spoilage).
- Grab-bag snacks: yogurt cup, jerky, cheese chunk, dark chocolate square.

## T1D workday notes
- Pre-bolus ~15 min before lunch (fixed desk schedule makes this easy) → flatter spike.
- ALWAYS keep fast carbs (glucose tabs) in your work bag — lifting lowers you and a busy day can hide a low.

## Rough daily protein (food + 1-2 whey scoops)
Each day lands ~180-190g: lunch protein ~45 + dinner ~50 + yogurt/cottage ~20-24 + eggs ~12-24 + whey ~25-50 + jerky/cheese ~10.
