# Research: Microplastics & Low-Tox Living (cited)

Deep-research pass, 2026-07-24. 30 sources fetched, 120 claims extracted, 25 adversarially verified (3-vote), 24 confirmed. Distinguishes real evidence from marketing.

## VERIFIED — high confidence

### Microplastics
- NEJM 2024 (Marfella et al.): carotid-plaque patients WITH micro/nanoplastics had 4.53x higher rate of MI/stroke/death (20% vs 7.5%). BUT observational ASSOCIATION, not causation; real limitations (possible surgical contamination, confounding). https://www.nejm.org/doi/full/10.1056/NEJMoa2309822
- Broader organ-harm claims = "suspected" from animal studies (rapid review: 28 animal / 3 human), not proven in humans. https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC11697325/
- Presence ≠ harmful dose: Taiwan fish risk assessment found hazard quotients <1.0, "no remarkable risk." https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC8540681/

### The proven intervention = reduce intake (NOT a detox product)
- Cutting food-contact plastic (fresh food in GLASS, no canned/packaged) cut urinary BPA 59-66% + phthalates 37-56% in 3-7 days. Diet = primary exposure route. Rudel 2011 https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC3223004/ ; PERTH Trial 2026 https://www.nature.com/articles/s41591-026-04324-7
- CAVEAT: only works with COMPLETE swap to genuinely uncontaminated food — one study saw phthalates RISE when "fresh" food (dairy/coriander) was itself contaminated.
- NO commercial "microplastic detox" product has evidence — presume marketing/scam. Lever = reduce intake.

### Personal care
- Swapping to phthalate/paraben/triclosan/oxybenzone-free products cut those biomarkers 27-45% in 3 days (HERMOSA). https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC5047791/
- ANTI-MARKETING FLAG: some "free-of" products RAISED other chemicals (one paraben +101%) via substitutes. READ FULL INGREDIENT LISTS, don't trust front-label claims. (Use Yuka app / EWG Skin Deep.)
- Parabens: genuinely unsettled — FDA says no proven harm at cosmetic doses (butylparaben ~10,000-100,000x weaker than estradiol); endocrine reviews flag concern. Reduce, don't panic. https://www.fda.gov/cosmetics/cosmetic-ingredients/parabens-cosmetics
- Triclosan: strongest "avoid" case (thyroid/skin-barrier); FDA already banned it from OTC antibacterial soaps 2016.
