40 HOOK TEMPLATES — SAI'S NICHE
================================

Audience: founders, entrepreneurs, agency owners, gen z builders,
hardworking 18–28 year olds chasing the founder path.

Identity: 21-year-old founder running an ad agency. Discipline-coded,
schedule-coded, anti-hustle-culture-but-pro-grind.

Voice rules baked in:
- "Founder" never "CEO"
- No dunking on peer founders — attack ideas, markets, or your past self
- No AI-flavored summary closers
- Keep concrete details (Mule #3, meditation, real ritual names)


VERBAL HOOKS — SAI EDITION
===========================

1. "Stop [ACTION] right now."
   Sai example: "Stop checking Slack before 10am."

2. "Nobody talks about [TOPIC]."
   Sai example: "Nobody talks about how lonely the founder path actually is."

3. "The biggest mistake I made [BUILDING X] was [MISTAKE]."
   (Self-targeted — avoids dunking on peer founders.)
   Sai example: "The biggest mistake I made building my agency was hiring
   too early."

4. "I'm going to save you [TIMEFRAME] of [PAIN]."
   Sai example: "I'm going to save you 2 years of trying to scale an agency
   the wrong way."

5. "[NUMBER] things I wish I knew before [MILESTONE]."
   Sai example: "5 things I wish I knew before signing my first $10k retainer."

6. "Here's the truth about [TOPIC] nobody will tell you."
   Sai example: "Here's the truth about agency life nobody on Twitter will
   tell you."

7. "I [OUTCOME] at [AGE] / in [TIMEFRAME]. Here's exactly how."
   Sai example: "I built a 6-figure agency at 21. Here's exactly how."

8. "Most people get [TOPIC] completely wrong."
   (Targets non-builders, not peer founders.)
   Sai example: "Most people get the founder grind completely wrong."

9. "POV: you're [AGE] and you just [MILESTONE]."
   Sai example: "POV: you're 21 and you just signed your first retainer."

10. "[AUTHORITY] told me [INSIGHT] and it changed everything."
    Sai example: "My first client told me one thing and it 10x'd my agency."

11. "Watch this before you [ACTION]."
    Sai example: "Watch this before you start an agency."

12. "The fastest way to [OUTCOME] is [UNEXPECTED THING]."
    Sai example: "The fastest way to grow your agency is firing clients."

13. "I tried [THING] for [TIMEFRAME]. Here's what happened."
    Sai example: "I tried a 5am wake-up for 30 days. Here's what changed."

14. "Why [COMMON BELIEF / IDEA] is actually wrong."
    (Attacks an idea, not peers.)
    Sai example: "Why hustle culture is actually wrong."

15. "Most [INDUSTRY] hits a ceiling at [POINT]. Here's how I broke it."
    (Solidarity, not a dunk.)
    Sai example: "Most agencies hit a ceiling at $20k MRR. Here's how I
    broke through."

16. "If you're a [ROLE] and not doing [THING], you're losing."
    Sai example: "If you're a founder and not blocking your calendar,
    you're losing."

17. "I used to [BAD HABIT] until I realized [INSIGHT]."
    Sai example: "I used to work 14-hour days until I realized something."

18. "Why does [DOING X AT Y] hit different?"
    (Reframed from punch-down — celebratory instead of accusatory.)
    Sai example: "Why does building a company at 21 hit different?"

19. "[A] vs [B] — which wins at [STAGE]?"
    Sai example: "Agency vs SaaS — which actually wins at 21?"

20. "Hot take: [POLARIZING OPINION ABOUT IDEA, NOT PEERS]."
    Sai example: "Hot take: most agency advice on Twitter is wrong."

3. EYE-LOCK CLOSE-UP

9. UNEXPECTED ACTIVITY

15. JUMP-CUT SAME-LINE

17. OUTFIT-SWAP BLINK EDIT

18. REACTION OVERLAY
    Sai execution: Sai's face in corner reacting to his MRR dashboard, a

19. PULL-OUT-OF-FRAME REVEAL
    Sai execution: Pull a printed contract out of frame: "This is what

20. NUMBER-ON-BODY TEXT
    Sai execution: "$40K MRR" written on chest in tape, or projected on

WHAT TO AVOID FOR SAI
======================

- "Most founders fail because…" — punches down at peers.
- "CEO" anywhere — he's a founder.
- Cute parenthetical asides like "(yes, really)" or "(spoiler: it didn't
  work)" — they sound AI-flavored, not Sai.
- Closing with "the lesson is…" or "what this comes down to is…" —
  synthesized closer energy.
- Generic "hustle harder" energy — he's discipline-coded, not hustle-coded.
