# 70 Hook Templates — Sai's Niche

**Audience:** founders, entrepreneurs, agency owners, gen z builders, hardworking 18–28 year olds chasing the founder path.
**Identity:** 21-year-old founder running an ad agency. Discipline-coded, schedule-coded, anti-hustle-culture-but-pro-grind.

**Voice rules baked in (from `feedback-sai-linkedin-voice.md`):**
- "Founder" never "CEO"
- No dunking on peer founders — attack ideas, markets, or your past self
- No AI-flavored summary closers
- Keep concrete details (Mule #3, meditation, real ritual names)

## Niche-Specific Themes To Plug Into Variables

When filling templates, draw from Sai's actual world — not generic founder-bro slop:

- **Schedule / discipline:** calendar blocking, 5am, meditation, Mule #3, deep work windows
- **Agency ops:** retainers, sales calls, client acquisition, hiring, firing, MRR, deliverables
- **Money milestones:** first $10k client, first $20k month, first $100k year, first hire
- **Identity:** 21yo founder, no degree (or whatever), gen z, NYC, building loud
- **Anti-narratives:** anti-hustle-culture (but pro discipline), anti-9-to-5, anti-wantrepreneur

## What To AVOID For Sai

- ❌ "Most founders fail because…" — punches down at peers (voice rule #4)
- ❌ "CEO" anywhere — he's a founder (voice rule #1)
- ❌ Cute parenthetical asides like "(yes, really)" or "(spoiler: it didn't work)" — AI-flavored (rule #2)
- ❌ Closing with "the lesson is…" / "what this comes down to is…" — synthesized closer energy (rule #3)
- ❌ Generic "hustle harder" energy — he's discipline-coded, not hustle-coded
