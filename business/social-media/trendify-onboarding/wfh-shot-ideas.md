# WFH Shot Ideas — Solo Filming Reference

For Trendify videos shot 100% remote, by one person at home (no team needed on camera).

---

## Screen-Based
- Screen recording of a doc, message, or app being used in real time
- Slack or email thread showing context
- Loom or video message being recorded at a desk
- Notion or Google Doc being updated live
- Calendar invite being sent
- Before/after — messy old doc transitioning to a clean updated version

---

## Talking Head (At Home)
- Direct to camera at a clean desk setup
- Reaction cam — reading something on screen, visibly reacting
- Over-the-shoulder showing the screen
- Close-up of face for emphasis lines

---

## B-Roll at Home
- Hands typing, scrolling, clicking through a document
- Phone screen showing a DM
- Coffee cup, laptop, notebook — classic WFH desk setup
- Light coming through a window across the desk
- Cursor hovering, clicking, dragging — clean and intentional

---

## Remote Collaboration
- Zoom call screenshot or screen recording (blur names if needed)
- Google Docs showing multiple cursors editing at once
- Calendar invite being sent to a creator
- Slack notification popping up

---

## Filming Tips
- Natural daylight beats ring lights for a real, warm feel
- Clean the desk before filming — viewers notice clutter
- Close extra tabs before screen recording (especially personal ones)
- Mic close to mouth (under-frame or lapel) for crisp audio
- Same outfit across multiple videos = looks like a series
