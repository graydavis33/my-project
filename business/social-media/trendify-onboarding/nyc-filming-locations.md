# NYC / Manhattan Filming Locations

Locations broken down by vibe for Trendify content.

---

## Creative / Editorial
- **Dumbo Bridge View** *(technically Brooklyn but 5 min away)* — iconic, cinematic
- **The Vessel at Hudson Yards** — futuristic, architectural
- **Highline** — elevated, urban, great natural light
- **Soho side streets** — cobblestone, clean storefronts, creative energy

---

## Professional / Startup Feel
- **Flatiron District** — glass buildings, walkable, modern
- **Bryant Park** — open, polished, good for outdoor talking head
- **World Trade Oculus** — dramatic interior, white architecture

---

## Relaxed / Human Feel
- **West Village streets** — warm, neighborhood feel, not corporate
- **Washington Square Park** — candid energy, real NYC
- **Ace Hotel Lobby** — stylish, communal, coffeeshop vibe

---

## Cinematic / Closing Shot Material
- **Brooklyn Bridge Park** *(quick uber)* — skyline backdrop
- **Top of the Rock or Edge** — NYC from above
- **Highline at golden hour** — warm tones, forward-looking feel

---

## Indoor / Architectural Without Filming at Home
- **NY Public Library Reading Room** — dramatic, serious, beautiful
- **Chelsea Market interior** — industrial creative, textured walls
