# Trendify Internal Onboarding Video Series

10-video onboarding series for new Trendify employees and clients. Each video ~30 seconds, mix of talking head and voiceover/B-roll, all shot solo by Sai (WFH).

## The Series

| # | Topic | Format |
|---|-------|--------|
| 1 | Welcome to Trendify | AI B-roll only + voiceover |
| 2 | Who We Are | Sai on camera, one take, no cuts |
| 3 | Tools We Use and How to Use Them | Screen recording walkthrough |
| 4 | How We Work | Screen recording walkthrough |
| 5 | How We Communicate | Split screen, Sai reacting to his own screen |
| 6 | The Creative Process | Documentary style, Sai at desk |
| 7 | What to Expect Week 1 | Voiceover + iPhone B-roll around his space |
| 8 | Team Culture | Candid clips + text overlays |
| 9 | Going Out of Office | Screen recording + talking head |
| 10 | What Success Looks Like | Cinematic AI B-roll, closing manifesto |

## Audience Goals
After watching, new employees and clients should feel:
- Excited
- Engaged
- Curious
- Knowledgeable on how things work
- Trust in the team
