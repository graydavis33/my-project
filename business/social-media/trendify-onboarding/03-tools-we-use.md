# Tools We Use and How to Use Them

---

## Slack

This is where most of our day-to-day communication happens. Keep things clear, thoughtful, and easy to follow.

- Channels are for shared context — DMs are for quick 1:1s
- A little clarity upfront goes a long way — helps people respond faster without back and forth
- Use threads to keep things organized and easy to track
- Keep it human — a little warmth and clarity makes a big difference

**Housekeeping:**

- **Profile Photo** — Must use the Trendify Orange background. Created by Ops during onboarding. Updates via [Canva template](https://www.canva.com/design/DAGqXT70p1Y/CSnKfTO5g51TM_WdX-EpPA/edit)
- **Notifications** — Keep ON at all times
- **Theme** — Customizable under Preferences → Appearance
- **Organizing Slack** — [Watch this video](https://youtu.be/TzYbvGAdolI)
   - Create Sections to group client channels and internal channels separately
   - Mark important DMs as "VIP" so they stay at the top of your sidebar

---

## Zoom

This is where most client calls and important meetings happen.

- Always record the call so nothing gets missed
- If you're leading, double check recording is on before starting
- Camera on, mic off unless you're speaking

---

## Google Meet

Mainly used for creator team calls.

- Join on time and ready to go
- Camera on preferred, mic off unless speaking

---

## Notion

Our home base — for now at least.

- Content assignments live in the creator & editor dashboard
- Keep your availability updated — it helps more than you think
- If something changes, update it here so no one's guessing
- If you're ever lost, just ask

---

## Notetakers

We use Notion Notetaker, Gemini, Zoom recordings, and Otter.

- If a call matters, turn this on
- Makes it easy to go back and stay aligned

---

## Google Drive

Where all files and deliverables live.

- Always use the correct client folder before uploading
- Permissions matter — only share with the right people
- Avoid public links unless needed
- When unsure, ask before sharing

---

## Motion

Where we track performance.

- Helps us see what's working and what's not
- If it's part of your role, check in regularly

---

## Loom

For explaining things without needing a meeting.

- If something's easier to show than type, record a quick Loom
- Before recording — close extra tabs, clean your screen
- Keep it simple, clear, and easy to follow

---

## Scribe

For documenting processes and building SOPs.

- If you're doing something more than once, it's worth turning into a Scribe
- Helps everyone follow the same flow
- Check it out: [scribe.com](https://scribe.com)

---

## Permissions — A Quick Note Across Everything

- Only share things with people who actually need access
- When in doubt, keep it restricted and ask
- A quick pause here saves a lot of cleanup later
