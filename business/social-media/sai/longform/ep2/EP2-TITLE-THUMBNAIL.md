# EP2 — Title & Thumbnail Ideas — "Systems Over Stress"

**Lock these BEFORE filming the A-roll** (Sai's rule). Title is **theme-only** — no episode number, no date. Grounded in the locked arc: the launch slips again → epiphany that *every stress is a missing system* → he builds the machine → underneath, it's really about people / the community he never had. Title/hook framings cross-checked against the founder-niche patterns from Sandcastles (curiosity + contrarian + emotional-story all land).

★ = my top picks.

## Title options (by angle)

**A. The lesson / clean declarative** (matches the contrarian-truth pattern)
- ★ Every Stress Is a Missing System
- Systems Over Stress
- I Stopped Grinding and Built Systems Instead

**B. The emotional core / people** (the strongest, most differentiated angle — the "why")
- ★ I Built a Company So I'd Have Friends
- It's All Just People
- The Social Life I Never Had

**C. The tension / story** (curiosity + stakes)
- The Week My Launch Fell Apart (Again)
- I Was Drowning, So I Built a Machine
- Nothing Went to Plan This Week

**D. Vulnerable / curiosity**
- ★ I Tie My Worth to Productivity — and It Almost Broke Me
- Why I Make Videos With Nothing to Sell

---

## My recommendation
Lead with **"I Built a Company So I'd Have Friends"** as the title-to-beat — it's the most differentiated, it's the true emotional spine (C2669), and it makes someone *click* without being clickbait. Hold **"Every Stress Is a Missing System"** as the strong runner-up if you want the lesson-forward framing. Test the two against each other.

Why these over the rest: the pure-lesson titles ("Systems Over Stress") are clean but a little expected; the people/loneliness angle is what no other founder doc is leading with, and it's the part of the episode that actually moves people.
