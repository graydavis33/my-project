# EP2 Interview Questions — Systems Over Stress

The A-roll sit-down questions, derived from the locked EP2 arc (`EP2-ARC-MAP.md`). The interview is the **spine** — Sai's answers are the narration; the week's B-roll cuts in under them. ★ = must-have (the story doesn't work without it). Film looser, "talking to a friend." Each block feeds a specific beat.

## Block A — The Intention / The Delay
Feeds: Week-Ahead + the season-long launch thread
- ★ Last week the plan was to finally turn the ads back on and launch the site. What actually happened — where does the launch stand right now?
- Why did it slip again, and how do you actually feel about it slipping?
- Walk me through what you decided to focus on instead this week.

## Block B — The Low Point / The Stress
Feeds: Act 1 (the mind-dump)
- ★ You said this week that you tie your self-worth to productivity. Unpack that for me.
- What does a genuinely bad day do to your head as a founder?
- You traced this one back to staying up for the Knicks game and skipping your night routine. Why does that routine matter so much?

## Block C — The Turn / The Epiphany
Feeds: The Theme + Act 2
- ★ You had an epiphany — "every stress in my life can be solved with a system." Say that again and explain what clicked.
- ★ You said structure is what frees your creativity. What do you mean? Aren't those opposites?
- You'd drifted from systems for a couple weeks. What pulled you back?

## Block D — The Machine
Feeds: Act 2 (the unglamorous work)
- Walk me through what you actually built this week — the editor workflow, the dashboards, the SOPs.
- ★ You handed off the onboarding call you used to run yourself. Why was that scary, and why does delegating matter so much right now?
- Why is an agency uniquely hard to build? (the "it's all people" / role-change example)

## Block E — The Why
Feeds: Act 3 (the emotional core)
- ★ You said building an agency is "just people," and that you love it. Where does that come from?
- ★ Tell me about UGA — the semester of college, not having friends because all you did was work.
- ★ The office you dream of — how is it the social life you never had?
- You surprised Gray with a new laptop this week. Why was that the instinct? (culture beat — optional, check the privacy flag)
- ★ You keep making these raw videos with nothing to sell. Why?

## Block F — The Cliffhanger
Feeds: Closer + next-week tease
- ★ The launch still hasn't happened. What has to be true for it to finally go live?
- What's the one thing you're most afraid of as you try to grow?

## Block G — Connective Lines (film these explicitly, short and clean)
Feeds: cold open, mid-doc rehook, closer card
- ★ Cold-open reset: "Last week the plan was to launch. That's not what happened."
- ★ Mid-doc rehook: "All of this — the systems, the SOPs — it's not about org charts. It's about people."
- Closer line: one quotable wisdom-drop for the outro card.
