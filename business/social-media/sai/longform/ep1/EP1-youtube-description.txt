Everyone glamorizes being a founder. Nobody shows you the part where a Fortune 500 client rejects your creators and gives you 24 hours to find new ones.

This is episode 1 of a weekly series where I show you everything that actually goes on behind the scenes of building Trendify, my bootstrapped ad agency, here in New York City. I am 21 years old. This week we turned our ads back on for the first time in over two years, the last time we did it our product failed and we shut the company down. No highlight reel, no filter, just the real day to day of running a company.

A few things I learned this week:
. Retention will always matter more than getting a ton of new customers
. Your hiring and training is an extension of your product, because people are the ones who execute on it
. You cannot build a company without pressure, you just have to be willing to endure it
. Things get hard, you just have to take it day by day
