# Sai — 5 Replicable Short-Form Formats (Sandcastles, 2026-06-17)

_Pulled from Sandcastles global search, last 30 days, high view counts + high outlier scores
(outlier = performance vs that channel's own average). Same niche (entrepreneur/founder/business),
deliberately **different formats** from Sai's raw diary vlog, all easy to replicate + iterate.
Chosen to span different muscles: decisive / intellectual / tactical / conversational / funny._

---

## 1. Rapid-fire decision framework — "keep or cut," answered fast
The single biggest outlier found. Punchy, decisive, rapid-fire business judgment calls.
- **7.8M views · 1,464x outlier** — @tommoneymays, "Talent without character is a liability…" (fire vs train)
- Watch: https://tiktok.com/@tommoneymays/video/7644299725675564318
- Breakdown: https://app.sandcastles.ai/video/057c2c7e-992d-4ac6-b05c-dd534a824408
- **Sai's version:** rapid-fire "good client vs bad client," "keep this ad or kill it," "hire or
  pass." He's made 10,000 ads — he can make these calls in half a second, and that *is* the content.

## 2. The contrarian insider reframe — "Ever notice how…"
Calm "here's how business actually works behind the curtain" truth bomb. Very high engagement (10–13%).
- **5.6M (IG) + 2.9M (TikTok)** — @aarondinin (Duke prof), "the most effective business models
  distract you from where the money really goes"
- Watch (IG): https://www.instagram.com/reel/DZGUkQiyOUn/
- Watch (TikTok): https://tiktok.com/@aarondinin/video/7646913614875544862
- Breakdown: https://app.sandcastles.ai/video/9cebd7ef-f823-4a5f-82cc-23f7332c6751
- **Sai's version:** "Ever notice how the best ads don't look like ads?" Pairs perfectly with the
  deep interview questions (formats-as-leverage, it's-all-people) — the answer becomes the script.

## 3. The "never say these words" language list — sharp do/don't list
A tight list of exact phrases/mistakes. Very saveable + shareable (Sai's stated priority).
- **6.5M views · 125x outlier** — @richard_hale_, "Never say these words in business"
- Watch: https://www.instagram.com/reel/DYxT-zQzMjm/
- Breakdown: https://app.sandcastles.ai/video/cf1b06bb-e9c5-4f00-9740-ffe2031ae9ac
- **Sai's version:** "Never say these words to a client," "5 phrases that instantly kill a
  marketing campaign," "words that make you sound junior."

## 4. Interview-a-high-status-person — ask money + mindset, fast cuts
Highest raw reach on the list. Short punchy interview clips that mine one person for wisdom.
- **17.8M views** — @theschoolofhardknockz interviewing Giannis ("how he got rich")
- Watch: https://www.instagram.com/reel/DYz3x9iC3Ux/
- Breakdown: https://app.sandcastles.ai/video/99ece1aa-cee2-436e-965c-0910f110143b
- **Sai's version:** direct home for the new interview format — flip it so **Sai is the
  interviewer** asking other founders/clients the elite open-ended questions, *or* he answers them
  in this fast-cut style. A proven, repeatable engine.

## 5. CEO / startup-life comedy skit — relatable founder humor
A different muscle entirely — relatability + humor about the absurdity of running a company. Very
shareable, low production.
- **1.3M views** — @mytechceo, "Less PR, more being a parole officer for his Twitter account" (#techceo)
- Watch: https://www.instagram.com/reel/DZKdKJuvjqR/
- Breakdown: https://app.sandcastles.ai/video/bcf9a2b1-d440-4252-a20f-e60cd5685770
- **Sai's version:** "POV: the client says 'make it pop,'" agency-life bits, the people on a
  founder's calendar. Adds levity to a feed that's mostly earnest.
