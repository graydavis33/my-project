## The 4 videos Sai should steal from

### 1. Fire vs Train — @tommoneymays
- Stats: 7.5M views, 1,412x outlier (biggest overperformer in the watchlist by far), TikTok
- Links: https://tiktok.com/@tommoneymays/video/7644299725675564318 | https://app.sandcastles.ai/video/057c2c7e-992d-4ac6-b05c-dd534a824408
- Why it held attention: ~12 rapid-fire hiring scenarios in 29 seconds, each with an instant verdict (Employee who does the minimum? Fire. Talented but toxic? Fire on the spot, ego is super expensive.). Each scenario is a tiny open loop, so a new payoff lands every ~2 seconds. No dead air. Ends on one quotable line: "Someday is not a day of the week."
- Hook formula: [Persona] who [does a thing]? [Instant verdict].
- Format: A-vs-B comparison, studio/office talking head.
- Sai's version: Hire vs Pass: UGC creators, or Keep vs Cut for agency hires. He's building/managing a team right now so it's authentic. Rapid-fire 10-12 real scenarios with an on-screen tally that flips as he speaks. End on his own quotable closer. Easiest to film: one-take talking head.

### 2. Free tool reveal (Claude + OpenBB = $25k Bloomberg terminal, free) — @koen_salo
- Stats: 345K views, 18.7x outlier, 3,229 comments, Instagram
- Links: https://www.instagram.com/reel/DZFISGHsvm7/ | https://app.sandcastles.ai/video/5d259ff3-0622-4362-838b-774a7d4101f8
- Why it held attention: a giant price anchor ($25,000) collapsed into "free + 4 dead-simple steps." The gap between expensive and free is the retention. The "Comment BLOOM and I'll send the link" CTA drove 3,000+ comments (algorithm boost).
- Hook formula: [Tool A] + [Tool B] = [expensive status symbol], completely free.
- Format: tutorial, text-on-screen. Weak spot flagged: passive static background — use a screen recording instead.
- Sai's version: [AI tool] + [AI tool] = a $10,000 creative team, completely free — show Trendify's actual AI ad-creation stack. Doubles as agency lead-gen. Keyword CTA (Comment STACK and I'll send the workflow). Pair with a screen recording of the tools in action.

### 3. Everything we shipped this week (build-in-public) — @jayhoovy
- Stats: build-in-public weekly recap, Instagram
- Links: https://www.instagram.com/reel/DZLZ82lqcqu/ | https://app.sandcastles.ai/video/d4bbea1e-9dd3-457c-8c97-89bc3e865b6f
- Why it matters most for Sai: it's the exact format of his weekly documentary, compressed into a short. Retention trick: every feature framed as "here's an annoyance you feel -> here's how we killed it," never just "here's a feature." Plus personality beats and a social-proof testimonial.
- Hook formula: This is everything we [shipped] this [week] for [project] that's going to [benefit].
- Sai's version: Everything we shipped this week at Trendify — a recurring short cut straight from the doc footage already being filmed. Frame each win as a client/creator pain it solves. Near-free content from existing material.

### 4. Business is not as hard as you think (reframe) — @hormozi
- Stats: 385K views, Instagram
- Links: https://www.instagram.com/reel/DZA5UTbBhXl/ | https://app.sandcastles.ai/video/86524543-34cc-4971-94a6-42213f1c0a64
- Why it held attention: names your excuse -> says "that's not the real reason" -> gives a simple 3-part framework. You stay to hear the real reason. Calm podcast-clip delivery, no flash.
- Hook formula: I'm pretty sure [thing] failed because [excuse]... actually it's [reframe].
- Sai's version: maps to the Episode 2 theme. "You think the problem is the ads. It's actually your systems." Or the season thread: "You think you need more clients. You actually need..." Talking head, problem -> reframe -> 3 things.

## The retention pattern underneath all four
Every winner opens a gap in the first 3 seconds and refuses to close it until the end (a price gap, a "real reason" reframe, or a stack of mini-verdicts). The viewer stays to resolve the tension. All are short, list-structured, no dead air, one quotable closer.

Two most copyable for Sai now:
- #1 Fire vs Train — a 5-minute one-take he's qualified to make right now.
- #3 Shipped this week — just a recut of footage already being filmed.
