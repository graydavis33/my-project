# TikTok SOP

**Production tier:** LOW (raw UGC, strip-down edit)
**Cadence:** Bundled with the daily 5-10+ UGC posts. TikTok gets every short.
**Owner:** Sai films → Gray edits → Gray posts

---

## Strategy (TikTok-specific)

TikTok rewards volume + on-platform engagement. Algorithm distributes each new video independently to fresh non-followers. Trial-reels-style behavior is the default on TikTok — every post is a fresh test.

- **Daily target: every UGC clip Sai sends goes to TikTok.** No filtering.
- **Different videos, not hook variants.** Each clip is its own body + hook + take.
- **No B-roll, no music, no graphics.** Pure UGC.
- Hook in the first 1-2 seconds. Sai's framing — not "this is what I learned today" but "here's the mistake I almost made today."
- 30-90 seconds optimal. Under 30s is fine for sharp takes.

---

## Filming (Sai)

- Vertical, phone, available light
- Talk to camera directly, eye contact
- Often mid-activity (cooking, walking, getting dressed, brushing teeth) — adds visual texture for free
- 30-90 seconds
- Drop into Karra Media GDrive `/Sai UGC drops/` (or whatever folder we settle on)

---

## Editing (Gray, ~3 min)

- [ ] Pull file from drop folder
- [ ] Trim head and tail dead space
- [ ] Cut internal silences / restarts / um-pauses (Premiere or CapCut, doesn't matter)
- [ ] Add **title at top** — disappears at 5s. Plain text overlay.
- [ ] Add **captions on bottom** — auto-generate, scan for errors, ship
- [ ] **NO B-roll. NO music. NO graphics. NO HyperFrames assets.** This is the explicit bet.
- [ ] Pre-post checklist gate (`pre-post-checklist.md`)
- [ ] Export 1080×1920, H.264, ~30Mbps

---

## Posting

- **Caption:** Sai-voice (see [[sai-linkedin voice revisions]] for what to avoid — em-dashes, fabricated 2-3-beat parallels, AI-essay headers, poetic verbs, etc.). For TikTok, captions are short. One sharp line beats three sentences.
- **Hashtags:** 3-5 max, niche-specific. No spammy stacks.
- **Cover frame:** pick a clear face frame from the first 1s.
- Post directly to TikTok (no auto-cross-poster — different timing optimizations per platform).

---

## Tracking

- Views, follow-through rate, completion rate, follows-from-video
- The metric that matters: **views-to-followers conversion %**
- Weekly: pull TikTok analytics into the social-media-analytics sheet (existing pipeline)
- Build a content-alignment thesis from the top 3 + bottom 3 each week

---

## Don't

- Don't add visuals to make it look "produced" — that's a regression
- Don't post Sai's IG Reels edit as TikTok unedited — TikTok is its own platform
- Don't post the same body with different hooks — Trial Reels is the IG mechanism; on TikTok every post is its own video
- Don't optimize for virality. Optimize for the pre-post checklist + conversion %
