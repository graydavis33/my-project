# Pre-Post Checklist

**Run every piece of content through these four questions before it ships.**

If yes to all four → ship.
If no on any → revise or kill the post.

**Performance is secondary to passing the checklist.**

---

## The Four

### 1. Honest?
Did I create out of truth — or am I chasing virality?

- The hook is a question I actually thought about, not a manufactured curiosity gap
- The take reflects what I really believe right now, not what I think will perform
- I'm not performing certainty I don't have

### 2. Craft?
Did I try to get better at the medium?

- The framing is sharper than the last one
- The hook lands faster
- The edit is tighter
- The thumbnail is cleaner than yesterday's
- This is incrementally better than what I made last week

### 3. Value?
Is what I said actually useful to someone watching this?

- A specific takeaway someone could act on
- A reframe that changes how someone thinks about something
- An honest account of a mistake or learning
- A story that lands a lesson, not just a story

### 4. True?
Did the thing I'm describing actually happen?

- The numbers are real
- The events happened in the order I described
- The lesson came from this experience, not a manufactured arc
- I'm not bending the story to fit a thesis

---

## How to apply

- **Daily UGC shorts:** Self-check in ~30 seconds before posting. Most should pass.
- **Long-form / ads / LinkedIn:** Sai reviews against the checklist; Gray flags any failed gate before publish.
- **When in doubt:** kill it. Volume is the goal but only of stuff that passes — failed-checklist content trains the wrong patterns.
