My only goal in middle school was to make my 7th grade basketball team. For two years straight, I showed up to every intramural run, every weight training class, and would spend hours after school going to the rec center to practice. When I tried out for the team, I got cut and I was ready to give up. The same coach who cut me told me to not give up and keep practicing for next year. He pushed me to join weight training, he would call me out every time I missed an intramural, and he saw something in me that I didn't even see in myself. And in 8th grade, I made the team. Coach Waddell believed in me when I didn't even believe in me. I had everything in order to succeed, but I just lacked the self-belief. Sometimes you just need someone to believe in you more than you believe in yourself. So find yourself a Coach Waddell and be the Coach Waddell for others.
