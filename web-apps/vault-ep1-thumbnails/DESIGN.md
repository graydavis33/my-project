# Vault EP1 — Thumbnail Concepts (DESIGN)

3 static 1280×720 YouTube thumbnail mockups for "The Vault — EP1" (Bezos → 2 hrs/week with customers → Sai implements in Trendify, 30-day clock).

Grounded in:
- The 30-thumbnail teardown (MrBeast / Hormozi / Jesse) — face+emotion, 1–4 huge words, visual conflict, one idea, curiosity gap.
- `business/social-media/sai/longform/yt-vault-series/VAULT-EP1-TITLE-THUMBNAIL-REFERENCE.md` — 323K-video CTR study: faces +36% in business, multi-face beats single, text costs −19% unless ≤10 chars, winning colors include orange, brightness 100–110, clean+big-number is the 2026 winner. Jordan Welch transformation-split = proven #1 lane.

## The 3 lanes (one per proven pattern)
- **A — Transformation split "DAY 1 | DAY 30"** → the data #1 lane (Jordan Welch 2.5M). Conflict = desaturated chaos vs saturated order.
- **B — Vault + dual-face + "$100B"** → MrBeast stakes/spectacle. Dual-face (+36%) + big anchor number.
- **C — The Wheel "I DIDN'T CHOOSE"** → the white-space differentiator (no competitor has the wheel) + Hormozi bold-text.

## Colors
- `#F28129` brand orange (accent / highlight / heat) — a proven winning thumbnail color
- `#FF9F45` orange-bright (glow core)
- `#0E1016` ink (canvas base) — kept mid-bright with glows, not crushed black
- `#2C313D` / `#3C4456` steel (vault, structure)
- `#F2EEE4` paper (files, light side)
- `#E8EDF5` near-white (text)

## Typography
- **Anton** — display/anchor text (condensed ultra-bold = the YouTube-thumbnail standard)
- **Montserrat 800** — badges, kickers, labels

## What NOT to do
- No sentences. Anchor text ≤10 chars, one idea.
- No crushed black — keep brightness ~100–110 with glows.
- No clutter — max one face-pair + one prop + one number per thumbnail.
- Faces are styled PLACEHOLDER plates — Gray drops in real Sai/Bezos cutouts.
