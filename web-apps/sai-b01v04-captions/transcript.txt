I hired someone for the first time at the age of 18 and it went horrible. That's because I didn't understand how to build proper systems while scaling. Step one, do an audit of everything that you do as a founder on a daily basis. Step two, document everything that you do step by step so even a fifth grader can understand how to do it. Step three, hire someone and walk them through the documentation. Step four, have them shadow you while you do the specific tasks so they understand how you do it. Step five, they do the task with your oversight so they can ask any questions. Step six, they do the task without you. If you nailed steps one and two, then they will nail step six. Then you just repeat this framework for every new hire in every area of the business.
