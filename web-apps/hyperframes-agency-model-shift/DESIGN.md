# Agency Model Shift — Visual Identity

## Style Prompt

Modern dashboard infographic. Crisp white linework on chroma green for chromakey production in Premiere. White-outlined cards, geometric SVG icons, single accent color (cyan) for emphasis and connection lines. Calm, confident motion — power3.out entrances, sine.inOut crossfade transitions for seamless flow between scene beats. Reads as the visual equivalent of a clear founder talking through a model on a whiteboard, but rendered as a polished modern UI.

## Colors

- Background (chromakey): `#00B140` chroma green
- Primary line/text: `#FFFFFF` white
- Card translucent fill: `rgba(255,255,255,0.04)`
- Accent (connections, dots, emphasis): `#06B6D4` cyan
- Idea highlight (Scene 3 only): `#FBBF24` amber

## Typography

- Display: Montserrat 700 / 600 — uppercase headlines and labels
- Numbers: Montserrat 700 with `font-variant-numeric: tabular-nums`

## Motion

- Entrances: `power3.out` (cards, headlines), `back.out(1.6)` (icons), `sine.out` (rings)
- Transitions: 0.55s `sine.inOut` crossfade — seamless flow
- Hold each scene 3.5–5s so the eye reads it before transition
- Final scene allowed to fade out

## What NOT to Do

- No green text or icons inside the asset (would key out with the BG)
- No gradients on thin lines (chromakey banding)
- No exit animations between scenes — transitions handle handoff
- No more than one accent color per scene (cyan everywhere except amber on Scene 3)
- No emoji or stock illustration — geometric SVG only
